How It Works

Simple, seamless, and designed to fit into your life

01

Create or join a group

Start a private circle with family, friends, or any group you care about. Invite members via email.

02

Receive thoughtful prompts

Get curated questions delivered on your schedule. Choose from our library, or create your own.

03

Share your updates

Respond to prompts whenever it’s convenient. Write as much or as little as you’d like.

04

Get a beautiful newsletter

Everyone’s updates are compiled into a formatted newsletter delivered the the whole group.

Example Prompts

From casual check-ins to deeper reflections, our prompts help spark meaningful conversations

Light & Fun

  • What’s your favourite new movie or TV show?
  • Share a photo that made you smile this week
  • What’s the best meal you’ve had recently?

Reflective

  • Share a happy moment from the past week
  • What’s something you’re proud of lately?
  • What are you looking forward to?

Deep Dive

  • What’s a lesson you’ve learned recently?
  • How are you taking care of yourself right now?
  • What’s challenging you right now?

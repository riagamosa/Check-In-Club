Frequently Asked Questions

No question is too small when it comes to staying connected. Here's everything you need to know about Check In Club.

How does Check In Club work?

You create or join a private group with the people you want to stay connected with. At a set interval, everyone in the group receives a curated prompt to respond to. Once all responses are in, everyone gets a beautifully formatted email compiling the whole group's answers in one place.

Who can see my responses?

Your responses are only visible to the members of your private group — no one outside of your group can see what you share.

How often will I receive check-ins?

Check-in frequency is set by the group, so you and your people get to decide what works best for everyone.

Can I be a part of more than one group?

Absolutely. You can join or create multiple groups — whether that's one for family, one for friends, or one for a community you're part of.

When will Check In Club be available?

We're still in development, but we're working hard to bring Check In Club to life. Sign up on our Sign Up page and we'll notify you the moment we launch.
